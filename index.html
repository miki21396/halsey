About

Halsey is an american pop singer. She was born in Edison, New Jersey, on September 29, 1994.
On January 27, 2021, Halsey announced that she is pregnant and expecting a child with Alev Aydin.
Ender Ridley Aydin was born on July 14, 2021.
In 2016, she was featured on The Chainsmokers' single "Closer", which topped the charts in over 10 countries, and was later certified 14× Platinum by the RIAA.
Her second studio album, Hopeless Fountain Kingdom (2017), consisted of more "radio-friendly" music than her previous releases; its singles "Now or Never" and "Bad at Love", both reached the top 20 of the Billboard Hot 100, with the latter peaking in the top five. In 2018, she released the single "Eastside" alongside Benny Blanco and Khalid, which reached the top ten on the Billboard Hot 100.
